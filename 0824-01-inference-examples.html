Microsoft Copilot Examples

Focus: Productivity, Business Tasks, and Microsoft 365 Integration

  1. Email Drafting: "Draft a professional email to my team of 5 people, informing them that the project deadline for 'Q3-Report' has been moved up to this Friday, October 27th. Mention that I understand this is a tight turnaround and that I'm available to help with any roadblocks. Keep the tone encouraging."
  2. Meeting Summary: "I have the following transcript from a meeting. Summarize the key decisions made and list the action items, assigning each to the correct person: [Transcript Text Here]."
  3. PowerPoint Outline Creation: "Create a 7-slide outline for a presentation on the 'Benefits of Remote Work'. The slides should cover an introduction, improved work-life balance, cost savings for employees and employers, increased productivity, challenges and solutions, and a conclusion."
  4. Excel Formula Help: "I have a table with 'Salesperson' in column A, 'Region' in column B, and 'Total Sales' in column C. What Excel formula can I use to find the total sales for the 'North' region?"
  5. Code Explanation (C#): "Explain what this C# code does in simple terms. It's for a .NET application. public class Calculator { public int Add(int a, int b) { return a + b; } }"
  6. Learning a New Skill: "I want to learn how to use Microsoft Power BI. Create a 30-day learning plan for me, starting from the basics and moving to more advanced topics like DAX formulas and data modeling."
  7. Idea Generation: "Brainstorm five creative marketing campaign ideas for a new brand of eco-friendly coffee cups."
  8. Document Summarization: "Summarize the main points of this 3-page policy document on corporate travel. Focus on the new reimbursement limits and the pre-approval process. [Document Text Here]."
  9. Image Creation for Presentation: "Create an image for a presentation slide about 'Global Collaboration'. It should be professional and feature a world map with interconnected nodes."
  10. Rewriting for Tone: "Rewrite this sentence to be more formal and persuasive: 'We should probably think about using the new software.'"
  11. Travel Itinerary: "Plan a 3-day business trip itinerary for a trip to London. Include flights from JFK, a hotel near the Canary Wharf, and recommendations for two client dinners."
  12. Preparing for a Job Interview: "I have a job interview for a 'Product Manager' role. Generate a list of 10 common questions they might ask me and provide bullet points on how to answer each one."

Google Gemini Examples

Focus: Multimodality, Creative Tasks, and Google Ecosystem Integration

  1. Video Analysis: "Summarize this 10-minute YouTube video about the history of the Eiffel Tower: [YouTube Link]."
  2. Image-to-Text: "I have a picture of a handwritten recipe. Transcribe the ingredients and instructions into a formatted list."
  3. Trip Planning: "Plan a 5-day family vacation to Rome. Create a day-by-day itinerary with kid-friendly activities and suggest routes using Google Maps."
  4. Google Docs Helper: "Read the provided Google Doc and suggest 3 ways to make the introduction more engaging."
  5. Creative Writing: "Write a short story from the perspective of a single raindrop during a thunderstorm in New York City."
  6. Complex Explanation: "Explain the concept of quantum entanglement to a high school student using a simple analogy."
  7. Visual Brainstorming: "Generate a mind map of ideas for a science fair project on renewable energy."
  8. Language Learning: "I'm learning Japanese. Create a practice conversation between a tourist and a shopkeeper, including romaji and English translations."
  9. Data Interpretation: "I have an image of a bar chart showing website traffic over 6 months. Describe the trend and identify the month with the highest traffic."
  10. Gmail Response: "Draft a polite and concise reply to a customer complaint email, acknowledging their issue and promising a follow-up within 24 hours."
  11. Code Generation (Python): "Write a Python script that uses the Google Calendar API to find all events happening next week."
  12. Ethical Dilemma: "Discuss the ethical implications of using AI to generate art. Present arguments from both the artist's and the technologist's perspective."

Claude Examples

Focus: Conversational Fluency, Creative Writing, and Coding/Debugging

  1. Natural Dialogue: "Let's have a conversation about the pros and cons of space exploration. I'll start: I think it's a huge waste of money. Convince me otherwise."
  2. Creative Collaboration: "Help me write a poem. I'll give you the first line, and you provide the second, and we'll alternate. First line: 'The city sleeps beneath a velvet shroud.'"
  3. Code Debugging: "Here is a piece of JavaScript code that's not working. The console shows a 'TypeError'. Can you find the bug and explain how to fix it? [Code Snippet]."
  4. Refactoring Code: "This Python function is functional but messy. Can you refactor it to be more readable and efficient? [Code Snippet]."
  5. Summarization without Bias: "Summarize the attached news article from a neutral, unbiased perspective, focusing only on the verifiable facts."
  6. Character Development: "Help me develop a character for a fantasy novel. He's an old, grumpy wizard who secretly loves to bake. What are his motivations and fears?"
  7. API Documentation: "Explain how to use the requests library in Python to make a POST request with a JSON payload. Provide a clear code example."
  8. Philosophical Discussion: "What is consciousness? Discuss the concept from both a scientific and a philosophical viewpoint."
  9. Drafting a Script: "Write a short, two-minute scene for a play between two old friends who haven't seen each other in 10 years and meet by chance in a park."
  10. Legal Text Simplification: "Translate this paragraph from a legal document into plain English that anyone can understand: [Legal Text]."
  11. Role-playing: "Act as a career coach. I'm feeling stuck in my current job. Ask me questions to help me figure out what I should do next."
  12. Test Case Generation: "Given this function that calculates the factorial of a number, write a set of unit tests to cover the main use cases and edge cases."

ChatGPT Examples

Focus: Complex Reasoning, Problem-Solving, and Versatility

  1. Logic Puzzle: "There are three boxes, one with apples, one with oranges, and one with a mix of both. All three boxes are labeled incorrectly. You can only pick one fruit from one box to figure out how to label them all correctly. Which box do you pick from?"
  2. Scientific Explanation: "Explain how CRISPR gene editing works at a molecular level. Use analogies to make it understandable for a non-scientist."
  3. SQL Query Generation: "Write an SQL query to find all users who signed up in the last 30 days and have made more than 5 purchases."
  4. Historical Simulation: "If you were a strategic advisor to Julius Caesar, what advice would you have given him before he crossed the Rubicon?"
  5. Business Plan Outline: "Create a detailed business plan outline for a subscription box service for dog toys. Include sections for market analysis, marketing strategy, and financial projections."
  6. Text-Based Adventure Game: "Start a text-based adventure game where I am a detective in 1940s Los Angeles. Give me my first case."
  7. Recipe Creation: "Create a recipe for a vegan lasagna that is also gluten-free. List the ingredients and provide step-by-step instructions."
  8. Complex Analogy: "Create an analogy to explain how a blockchain works, using the concept of a shared, public notebook."
  9. Travel Planning: "I have 7 days to travel in Italy. I want to visit Rome and Florence. Create a balanced itinerary that includes travel time, major sights, and food recommendations."
  10. Code Optimization: "Here's a slow Python script for data processing. Identify the performance bottlenecks and suggest ways to optimize it."
  11. Essay Brainstorming: "Brainstorm three different thesis statements for an essay about the impact of social media on modern politics."
  12. SAT Math Problem: "A store sells a shirt for $15. They have a sale for 20% off. If the sales tax is 8%, what is the final cost of the shirt? Show your work."

Grok Examples

  1. Recent News: "What was the biggest news story in the tech world in the last 24 hours? Summarize it for me and include a link to a relevant article."
  2. Social Media Trends: "What's the latest meme trending on X (formerly Twitter) right now? Describe it and explain why it's popular."
  3. Sarcastic Response: "Explain to me why waking up at 5 AM is a good idea, but do it in a sarcastic tone."
  4. Live Sports: "Who is currently winning the Manchester United game, and what was the most recent major play?"
  5. Witty Product Description: "Write a product description for a coffee mug that says 'I'm silently correcting your grammar.' Make it funny and slightly condescending."
  6. Stock Market Info: "What was the closing price of Tesla (TSLA) stock today, and what has been the general trend this week?"
  7. Movie Reviews: "What are people on X saying about the new 'Dune' movie? Give me a summary of the general sentiment."
  8. Generate a Tweet: "Write a tweet in the style of Elon Musk announcing a new, ridiculously ambitious feature for X."
  9. Humorous Advice: "Give me some terrible life advice, but present it as if it's the best wisdom anyone has ever heard."
  10. Real-time Fact Check: "A politician just claimed that unemployment has gone down by 10% this month. Is there any data to support or refute this claim in real-time?"
  11. Roast Me: "Roast my bio: 'Lover of coffee, code, and oxford commas.' Be witty but not too mean."
  12. Comparing Recent Events: "Compare the public's reaction on social media to the latest Apple product launch versus the latest Google product launch."
